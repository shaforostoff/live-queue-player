Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

1. Introduction

This application (“Pocket Tandas”) is a music player designed to play audio files stored locally on the user’s device. We take user privacy seriously and are committed to being transparent about how Pocket Tandas handles data.

2. Data Collection and Use

Pocket Tandas does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data.

Specifically, Pocket Tandas does not access or collect:

  • personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.);
  • device identifiers;
  • location data;
  • usage analytics or statistics;
  • advertising identifiers;
  • any data unrelated to local audio playback.

3. Permissions

Pocket Tandas may request access to device storage solely for the purpose of reading audio files that the user chooses to play. This access is used locally on the device only and no information is transmitted to any server or third party.

4. Third-Party Sharing

Pocket Tandas does not share any data with third parties, advertisers, analytics providers, or external services.

5. Security

Because Pocket Tandas does not collect or store any data, there is no risk of data leakage or unauthorized access related to user data.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
